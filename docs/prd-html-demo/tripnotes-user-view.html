Nonbei Yokocho
"Drunkard's Alley" - 60 tiny bars crammed into 2 narrow alleys. Each seats maybe 6 people. Order beer and whatever the person next to you is having.
📍 2 min from Shibuya Station, East Exit
Evening
Shibuya Sky
Skip Tokyo Tower. This rooftop has 360° views and stays open until 11pm. Go at blue hour (30 min after sunset) for magic lighting.
📍 Top of Shibuya Scramble Square • ¥2,500 📸 2 photos

Chureito Pagoda Secret
Everyone goes at noon - worst time! Go at 3pm when tour buses leave. Golden hour at 4pm in spring. 398 steps but worth every one.

Real Tsukiji Experience
Skip the famous sushi lines. Go to Tsukiji Uogashi - the new wholesale building. 2nd floor has restaurants where actual workers eat. Half the price, twice as good.
📍 Next to Tsukiji Station
